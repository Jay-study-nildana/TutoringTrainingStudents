Welcome

Ameesha Patel (pronounced [əˈmiːʂa pəˈʈeːl]), (born Amisha Amit Patel; 9 June 1975), is an Indian actress and model who predominantly appears in Hindi films alongside few Telugu films. She made her acting debut in 2000 with the romantic thriller film Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai which became critically and commercially successful and won her several awards including Zee Cine Award for Best Female Debut. Patel rose to prominence with Gadar: Ek Prem Katha (2001), which became one of the biggest hits in the history of Hindi cinema,[2] earning her a Filmfare Special Performance Award. She made her Telugu film debut with the successful Badri (2000).
